Pan Roasted Chicken Thighs (Batch Protein)

Ingredients

  • 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • Kosher salt & black pepper
  • 1 bunch cilantro, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • Rosemary (2 sprigs), Thyme (3 sprigs), finely chopped
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Pinch red pepper flakes
  • Juice & zest of 2 lemons
  • ¼–½ cup olive oil (to form paste)
  • 1 cup water (for pan)

Directions

  1. Pat chicken dry; season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Mix cilantro, garlic, shallots, rosemary, thyme, paprika, cumin, red pepper flakes, lemon juice & zest. Whisk in oil to a spreadable paste.
  3. Coat chicken; arrange in baking dish. Add ~1" water to pan (don’t submerge chicken).
  4. Roast at 400°F for ~35 min, until 165°F internal. Rotate pan if needed.
  5. Cool, then shred meat; discard bones/skin. Reserve pan stock.

Chef’s notes: Marinate 1–2 hrs (or overnight) for deeper flavor. Shredded chicken is your base for the week. Use the stock to enrich grains, soups, and sauces.

Southwest Burrito Bowl

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked brown rice or quinoa
  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 1 cup black beans, 1 cup corn
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Pico: 2 tomatoes, ½ red onion, 1 clove garlic, ½ jalapeño, cilantro, lime (juice & zest), ½ tsp cumin, ½ tsp paprika, olive oil, salt & pepper

Directions

  1. Cook rice/quinoa (use chicken stock if available).
  2. Make pico de gallo; season to taste.
  3. Build bowl: rice → chicken → beans & corn → pico → avocado.
  4. Finish with lime crema (Greek yogurt + lime juice).

Chef’s note: Store components separately; assemble fresh for best texture.

Mediterranean Pita Wrap (Dill & Honey Yogurt)

Dill & Honey Yogurt

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Dill & parsley, chopped (handful each)
  • 2 Tbsp honey
  • Zest & juice of 2 lemons
  • Salt & pepper

Wrap & Build

  1. Whisk yogurt sauce (loosen with lemon juice).
  2. Warm pitas; spread sauce.
  3. Layer chicken, cucumber, tomato, red onion, parsley, feta. Fold & enjoy.

Chef’s note: Sauce doubles as dip or grain-bowl dressing. Assemble wraps fresh for best texture.

Asian Chicken Stir-Fry (Garlic–Ginger Soy Glaze)

Glaze

  • 2 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, 2 Tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 bunch green onion, chopped
  • ½ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp cornstarch + 2 Tbsp water (slurry)

Stir-Fry

  1. Sauté garlic & ginger in oil; add sugar to coat. Stir in green onion & soy; simmer.
  2. Whisk in slurry gradually to thicken.
  3. Stir-fry mixed veggies (3 cups: broccoli, bell pepper, carrots, snap peas) 3–4 min; season lightly.
  4. Add shredded chicken; heat through. Toss with glaze; garnish with sesame seeds & more green onion.

Chef’s note: Keep sauce thinner if storing—it thickens on reheat.

Chicken & Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 celery stalks, 2 carrots, 1 onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2–3 cups shredded chicken
  • Bay leaf, thyme (2–3 sprigs), rosemary (1 sprig)
  • 6 cups stock (reserved + water)
  • Optional: ½ cup rice or small pasta
  • Salt & pepper

Directions

  1. Sweat celery, carrot, onion, garlic in oil until soft.
  2. Add chicken & aromatics; pour in stock/water. Simmer gently.
  3. Add rice/pastina if using; cook until tender. Season; remove aromatics.

Chef’s note: 4 days in fridge, 2 months frozen. Reheat gently for tender chicken.

Cran-Walnut Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • ½ red bell pepper, ½ small onion (diced)
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • ¼ cup dried cranberries, ¼ cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 tsp Dijon, 1 tsp honey
  • Blackened seasoning, salt, pepper
  • 2–3 Tbsp mayo (or Greek yogurt, or 50/50)

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients; season to taste.
  2. Serve as sandwich filling, over greens, or as a protein snack.

Chef’s note: Balanced savory-sweet-crunchy; easy to meal prep.
